Good Reads

Science-Fiction Books

  1. The Time Machine: HG Wells

    The Time Machine is a book about time travel. The setting is 1890s England.

    The story follows a man as he travels through time, exploring the world.

    If you love science fiction novels this is a must read.



  2. Fahrenheit 451: Ray Bradbury

    Fahrenheit 451 is a dystopian novel by Ray Bradbury published in 1953.

    It is regarded as one of his best works.

    The novel presents a future American society where books are outlawed and "firemen" burn any that are found.

    This book centers around Guy Montag and his gradual change from burning books to saving them.

    The moment when Montag changes his views from good to bad was when he witnessed the burning of an old lady in her own home.

    This novel has won many awards and has become the beacon of hope for all censored material.



  3. Ender's Game: Orson Scott Card

    Ender's Game is a novel about a child that is turned military general.

    After aliens known as the buggers attack Earth, Ender is the only hope left at salvation.

    The entire book centers around Ender's struggle and the ending is one of the most amazing ones ever written.
